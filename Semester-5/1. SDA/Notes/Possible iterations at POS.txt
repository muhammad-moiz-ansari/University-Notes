Possible iterations at POS:
1. Customer arrives at a POS checkout with items to purchase
2. Cashier starts a new sale, start perparing invoice
3. Cashier enters item ID, or scan item bar code
4. Enter quantity  2 or more, default quantity is 1
cashier repeats steps 3 and 4, until all items are scanned
5. Cashier ask total net amount for payment (disc and tax)
6. Customer pays cash or by card
7. Invoice is generated with balance.
