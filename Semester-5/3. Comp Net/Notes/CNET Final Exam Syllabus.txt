CNET Final Exam Syllabus

1. Socket Programming (Week 5)
   1.1. Week 5: UDP and TCP Socket API (socket, bind, listen, accept, connect, sendto, recvfrom)

2. Transport Layer (Week 6-10)
   2.1. Week 6: Transport-layer Services, Multiplexing/Demultiplexing, and UDP
   2.2. Week 7: Principles of Reliable Data Transfer (rdt 1.0 - 3.0, Pipelining, GBN, SR)
   2.3. Week 8: Connection-Oriented Transport: TCP (Segment Structure, Flow Control, Connection Management)
   2.4. Week 9: Principles of Congestion Control
   2.5. Week 10: TCP Congestion Control and VLSM (Variable Length Subnet Masking)
   2.6. VLSM

3. Network Layer - Data Plane (Week 11-13)
   3.1. Week 11: Router Architecture, Buffer Management, and Packet Scheduling
   3.2. Week 12: IP Protocol (Datagram Format, Addressing, DHCP, NAT, and IPv6)
   3.3. Week 13: Generalized Forwarding and SDN (OpenFlow Match + Action)

4. Network Layer - Control Plane (Week 14-15)
   4.1. Week 14: Routing Algorithms (Link State/Dijkstra and Distance Vector)
   4.2. Week 15: Intra-ISP Routing (OSPF), Inter-ISP Routing (BGP), and ICMP

5. Link Layer (Week 16)
   5.1. Week 16: Link Layer Services, Error Detection/Correction, Multiple Access Protocols, and ARP (Up to Slide 50)

In essence, Week 5 to 16 (up till Slide 50) and VLSM.

Note: Topics after ARP are not included in the final syllabus.
